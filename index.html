How it works

From idea to campfire in three steps.

1

Tell NomadAI what you want

"Weekend near Sedona, free dispersed, RV-friendly." Or just "moab" — vague is fine, NomadAI asks for what's missing.

2

Pick the spots that fit

You get a curated mix from every source — Recreation.gov, BLM, dispersed, community picks. Save the ones you'd actually stay at.

3

Hit the road

NomadAI sequences your stops, routes road-by-road, and opens directions in Apple Maps when you're ready to head out.
